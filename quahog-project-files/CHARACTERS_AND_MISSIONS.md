# The Narrows — Characters & Missions Bible

Story design for **The Narrows**, an original open-world crime story set on the
**real Massachusetts South Coast** — New Bedford, Fall River, Brockton, Cape Cod.
Real places and real landmarks; **original characters**; historical/parody figures
where they fit. Tone: rust-belt noir with a salt-air sense of humor — *Vice City
feel, South Coast soul.*

> Naming canon: real geography + real landmark names + original cast. Parody
> district names (Cascade/New Sefton/etc.) are retired.

---

## Principal cast

### Vincent "Vinny" Tavares — the player
South Coast born, just back after a few years "away" (prison up north). Quick
hands, slow to trust, allergic to authority. Wants a stake of his own — a boat, a
bar, a name. Half Azorean, raised in the Flint; knows these streets in his bones.
Arc: deckhand errand-runner → made man → kingpin of the harbor.

### Allies / contacts
- **"Auntie" Conceição Rosa** — runs a South End bakery and the neighborhood's
  real economy. Vinny's fixer and conscience. (Also the voice of Maré Alta radio.)
- **Deacon Mealy** — defrocked, keeps the lamp lit at the old chapel; launders
  more than souls. Mission-giver out of **Seamen's Bethel**.
- **Reggie "Two-Stroke" Pina** — mechanic/chop-shop, your garage & wheels guy
  (Bronco, Mustang, the G, the Z, the RAV4 all pass through his bay).
- **Marisol Cabral** — Cape Verdean ex-Coast Guard, runs boats and doesn't miss.
  Romance/loyalty thread; the getaway driver of the sea.
- **Iron Mike Fontaine** — Brockton boxing legend turned trainer; the "Undefeated"
  thread. Teaches Vinny to take a hit and give a worse one. (Radio: The Anvil.)

### Antagonists
- **Sully Brangwynne** — corrupt harbormaster; controls who fishes, who pays.
  The first wall Vinny hits, the first he climbs.
- **Chip Worthington III** — Cape Cod money laundering dirty cash through a
  "heritage marina." Performative, preppy, dangerous underneath the boat shoes.
- **Lady Beatrice "Lady" Borden** — matriarch of a Fall River dynasty trading on
  an infamous name; runs the Highlands and the "Acquitted" thread from a Second
  Street manor. Cold, theatrical, untouchable — until she isn't.
- **The Fake Kennedys** — a clan of grifters in borrowed Hyannisport polish
  running political-favor and real-estate scams across the Cape. Comic-menacing.

### Factions / crews
- **The Azorean Syndicate** — old-country muscle out of the Flint (Fall River).
- **The South End Crew** — Cape Verdean street operation, New Bedford; lowriders,
  loyalty, turf.
- **Cape Set** — Worthington's preppy money + the Fake Kennedys; clean shoes,
  dirty money.
- **The Brass** — local PD + the harbormaster's office; the law you bend or break.

### Radio personalities (in-game now)
- **Buddy Mello** — *The Rage* (talk): bridge traffic, potholes, civic rage.
- **Iron Mike Fontaine** — *The Anvil* (sports): boxing gospel, City of Champions.
- **DJ Sully** — *WHALE 92.1* (classic rock).
- **Tia Conceição** — *Maré Alta 105.3* (Portuguese oldies).

## Mission arc

### Vertical slice (the opening — already the spawn point)
**"Off the Boat."** Vinny arrives at the **New Bedford fish pier**. Tutorial loop:
walk the cobblestones to **Seamen's Bethel** → meet Deacon Mealy → a deal at the
fish auction goes wrong → **ambush on the pier** → steal a car at Reggie's signal
→ **high-speed coastal-fog getaway** up the waterfront → reach the first safehouse.
Teaches: walk, enter/drive, evade, basic combat.

### Act I — The Whaling City (New Bedford)
1. **Auction Rules** — strong-arm Sully Brangwynne's collectors; learn the docks.
2. **South End** — earn the South End Crew's trust (a lowrider run; a feast-night
   favor at the Blessed Sacrament).
3. **Co-op** — take over a wharf operation; first owned property → income.
4. **Boss:** break Sully's grip on the harbor.

### Act II — Spindle City (Fall River)
1. **Up the Hill** — cross the **Braga Bridge** into the Flint; broker with the
   Azorean Syndicate.
2. **"Acquitted"** — the Lady Borden thread: a heist/cover-up run out of the
   **Second Street manor**; the dynasty's secret leverage.
3. **The Undefeated** — train with Iron Mike at **Champion City Gym** (Brockton);
   a fixed-fight subplot you can throw or win.
4. **Boss:** dismantle Lady Borden's hold on the Highlands.

### Act III — The Cape & the Storm
1. **Heritage Marina** — infiltrate Chip Worthington's laundering op.
2. **Fake Kennedys** — a long con against the grifter clan; political fallout.
3. **"Gloria"** — the mid/late set-piece: a hurricane (homage to '85) floods the
   low roads, alters the map, sinks boats — a chaos heist during the storm.
4. **Finale:** **Battleship Cove** — a final confrontation aboard/around the USS
   *Massachusetts*; Vinny takes the harbor for good (or loses everything).

### Side threads & activities
- **Property empire** — bars, wharves, the gym, a marina; daily income.
- **Boat smuggling runs** with Marisol (sea routes, Coast Guard heat).
- **Street races** across the Braga/Bourne bridges; **boxing** circuit.
- **Lizzie Borden ghost-tour** side caper; **clam-shack** delivery time-trials.
- **Radio call-ins** — Buddy Mello reacts to your rampages on The Rage.

## Systems hooks (ties to the build)
- **Spawn picker** spots map to story beats: New Bedford (Act I), Lizzie Borden &
  Fall River (Act II), Battleship Cove (Act III finale).
- **Wanted/heat**: dual-axis — **The Brass** (police) + **faction aggro** (whose
  turf you shot up).
- **Vehicles**: Reggie's garage stocks the Bronco/Mustang/G/Z/RAV4.
- **Weather**: the live weather cycle escalates into the scripted "Gloria" storm.
