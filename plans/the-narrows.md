## Setting (2026)

The game is set **now**: contemporary cars, phones, LED signage, and today's South Coast
streetscape on top of the real OSM map. The **Coastal Neon** look is a *lighting mood*
(wet dusk, sodium lamps, tavern glow, harbor fog) — not a period piece.

- Real towns: New Bedford, Fall River, Fairhaven, Brockton, Cape Cod corridor
- Parody district names (New Sefton, Cascade, etc.) remain **in-world flavor** in radio/signage
- Story tone: grounded open-world crime drama, satire in signage and radio
